## 🇳🇿 New Zealand

### Recommended Master's Programs

#### ✔️ Master of Information Technology (MOST RECOMMENDED)
- Covers full stack, cloud, backend
- High demand for graduates

#### ✔️ Master of Computer Science
- More theory + development roles

#### ✔️ Master of Software Engineering
- Best if available

### Top Universities (Safe Choices)
- University of Auckland
- Auckland University of Technology (AUT)
- Victoria University of Wellington
- University of Canterbury
- Massey University

**Note:** All offer IT / CS / Software programs

### Cost Breakdown
- **Annual Tuition (Data Science & IT):** NZD $30,000–$38,000/year
- **Annual Fees:** ₹13L–20L/year
- **Annual Living Cost:** ₹10–15L/year

### Entry Requirements
- **IELTS:** 6.5 overall (no band less than 6.0)
- **PTE Academic:** 58+

### Work Permissions
- **Part-time:** 20 hrs/week allowed
