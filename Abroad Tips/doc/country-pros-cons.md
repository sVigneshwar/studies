# Country Comparison - Pros & Cons

## 1. Canada

- **Studies Duration:** 2 years
- **Job Search Visa:** 3 years
- **Work Requirement for PR:** 12 months skilled work needed to apply PR afterwards
- **If No Work:** Leave country
- **Approx Cost:** ₹45 lakh (2 years)
- **Part-time Work:** Allowed

**Pros:**
- Good country overall
- Peaceful in small towns

**Cons:**
- Difficulty getting a job
- Many Indians (may affect job market)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. Ireland

- **Studies Duration:** 1 year
- **Job Search Visa:** 2 years (Stamp 1G)
- **Work Requirement for PR:** Critical Skills Permit from employer → 2 years work → Stamp 4 (nearly PR status)
- **If No Work:** Leave country
- **Approx Cost:** ₹27 lakh (1 year)
- **Part-time Work:** Allowed

**Pros:**
- Good country overall
- Good lifestyle
- Major tech hubs (English-speaking advantage vs other European countries)

**Cons:**
- Small country (limited opportunities)
- Many Indians in the country
- Safety concerns at night in some areas
- Becoming like next UK due to immigration
- Difficulty getting jobs; employers hesitant with visa sponsorship
- Low job vacancies + high competition

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Germany

- **Studies Duration:** 2 years
- **Job Search Visa:** 1.5 years
- **Work Requirement for PR:** EU Blue Card or Work Permit → PR timeline: ~21 months (with Blue Card + good salary + German) OR ~33 months (standard path)
- **If No Work:** Leave country
- **Approx Cost:** ₹20–25L (2 years)
- **Part-time Work:** Allowed

**Pros:**
- Strong IT job market
- Very low or free tuition (public universities)
- Safe country overall
- Good work-life balance
- Less immigration tension compared to Ireland/UK

**Cons:**
- German language requirement
- Slow bureaucracy (visa, paperwork)

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Australia

- **Studies Duration:** 1.5–2 years
- **Job Search Visa:** 2–4 years (Temporary Graduate Visa – subclass 485)
- **Work Requirement for PR:** Skilled job → PR pathways (points-based system)
- **If No Work:** Leave country
- **Approx Cost:** ₹45–60L (2 years)
- **Part-time Work:** Allowed (good earning potential)

**Pros:**
- Strong job market
- High salary
- Safe, multicultural society
- Large Indian community (support system)

**Cons:**
- Very expensive
- PR becoming harder (increasing point competition)
- Long commitment required

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 5. New Zealand

- **Studies Duration:** 1–2 years
- **Job Search Visa:** Up to 3 years post-study work visa
- **Work Requirement for PR:** Skilled migrant PR pathway
- **If No Work:** Leave country
- **Approx Cost:** ₹35–48L (1.5 years) OR ₹46–66L (2 years)
- **Part-time Work:** Allowed

**Pros:**
- Very safe country
- Peaceful lifestyle
- Easier PR than many other countries
- Family-friendly

**Cons:**
- Small job market
- Fewer tech roles

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 6. Austria

- **Studies Duration:** 2 years
- **Job Search Visa:** ~12 months (typical EU model)
- **Work Requirement for PR:** Red-White-Red Card (work permit)
- **If No Work:** Leave country
- **Approx Cost:** ₹18–25L (2 years)
- **Part-time Work:** Allowed

**Pros:**
- High quality of life
- Lower cost than Western Europe

**Cons:**
- German language required
- Smaller IT market

------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 7. Swiz

- **Studies Duration:** 1–2 years
- **Job Search Visa:** ~6 months job search period (very short)
- **Work Requirement for PR:** Employer sponsorship required
- **If No Work:** Leave country
- **Approx Cost:** ₹50–70L (2 years)
- **Part-time Work:** Allowed

**Pros:**
- Very high salary
- Extremely safe
- Best quality of life

**Cons:**
- VERY expensive
- Very hard immigration
- Limited visas available
- Employer sponsorship strict
